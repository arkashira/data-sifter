# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for Data-Sifter

### Target Integrations

1. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Enables users to automate workflows by connecting Data-Sifter with thousands of other apps, enhancing data extraction and analysis capabilities.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-add**: Streamlines user workflows by automating data transfer between platforms, saving time and reducing manual effort.

2. **Airtable**
   - **Rationale**: Provides a flexible database solution for users to store and organize scraped data in a user-friendly interface.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1,200 records per base, 2GB attachment space.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-add**: Enhances data management capabilities, allowing users to easily manipulate and visualize extracted data.

3. **Google Sheets**
   - **Rationale**: Offers a familiar spreadsheet interface for users to analyze and share scraped data collaboratively.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 15GB total storage across Google Drive.
   - **Integration effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-add**: Facilitates easy data manipulation and sharing, making it accessible for users who prefer spreadsheet tools.

4. **Slack**
   - **Rationale**: Enables real-time notifications and updates on scraping tasks, improving team collaboration and responsiveness.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 10,000 messages in search, 10 integrations.
   - **Integration effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-add**: Keeps teams informed and engaged with data extraction processes, enhancing communication around data insights.

5. **Notion**
   - **Rationale**: Allows users to document and organize insights from scraped data in a collaborative workspace.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Unlimited pages and blocks, 5 guests.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-add**: Provides a comprehensive platform for users to manage and share insights, improving knowledge retention and collaboration.

6. **PostgreSQL**
   - **Rationale**: A powerful relational database for users who require robust data storage and querying capabilities for large datasets.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Varies by provider (e.g., Heroku offers 1,000 rows).
   - **Integration effort**: Large (L)
   - **Value-add**: Supports complex data analysis and reporting, catering to users with advanced data needs.

7. **HubSpot**
   - **Rationale**: Integrating with HubSpot allows users to automate marketing and sales processes based on data insights gathered from web scraping.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 2,000 contacts, 1 user.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-add**: Enhances lead generation and customer engagement efforts by leveraging scraped data for targeted marketing.

8. **Twilio**
   - **Rationale**: Enables users to send SMS or notifications based on data events or triggers from Data-Sifter.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 500 SMS messages.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-add**: Provides real-time communication capabilities, allowing users to act quickly on data insights.

### Prioritization Criteria
- **Affiliate/Revenue-share potential**: Target partners with affiliate programs to monetize integrations.
- **User job alignment**: Focus on integrations that directly address user pain points and enhance the overall value of Data-Sifter.
- **Integration effort**: Balance between quick wins (S) and more complex integrations (L) to ensure a steady development pipeline.
