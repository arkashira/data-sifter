# Customer Journey Map for Data Sifter

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developer encounters difficulties in gathering data from multiple websites for a project.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness of available tools; existing solutions are either too complex or not tailored for specific needs.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, and a sense of urgency to find a solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Create engaging content (blogs, tutorials, webinars) showcasing the ease of use and effectiveness of Data Sifter; leverage SEO to ensure visibility.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and content engagement rates.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developer starts researching potential solutions and compares different web scraping tools.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options; difficulty in understanding the unique benefits of Data Sifter compared to competitors.
- **User Emotions**: Skepticism, cautious optimism, and a desire for clarity.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer clear comparison charts, user testimonials, and case studies that highlight success stories; provide a demo video to illustrate ease of use.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and time spent on comparison pages.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Developer decides to try Data Sifter after positive research findings.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; potential technical issues during the trial phase.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful but anxious about the tool’s performance and usability.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide an intuitive onboarding process with guided tutorials; offer responsive customer support during the trial period.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user feedback on onboarding experience.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Developer successfully completes the trial and decides to integrate Data Sifter into their workflow.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about pricing and ongoing support; potential integration challenges with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Relief and satisfaction, but also apprehension about long-term commitment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer flexible pricing plans and a dedicated account manager for personalized support; provide integration resources and best practices.
- **Metric**: Subscription conversion rate and customer satisfaction scores.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Developer realizes the potential of Data Sifter for additional projects or team use.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about scaling usage; potential resistance from team members unfamiliar with the tool.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement about new possibilities, but also concern about team buy-in and resource allocation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Share advanced features and use cases; offer team training sessions and incentives for referrals.
- **Metric**: Increase in user accounts and feature adoption rates.
