# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
Data Sifter is a web scraping and data extraction tool designed for developers, enabling them to efficiently gather and analyze data from multiple websites with ease.

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**
1. Data Sifter empowers developers to automate data extraction, transforming the way they interact with online information.
2. Streamline your data analysis process with Data Sifter, the ultimate tool for developers to extract insights from any website.
3. Unlock the potential of web data with Data Sifter, a user-friendly solution for developers seeking to enhance their data-driven decisions.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Alex the Data Analyst**
   - **Daily Job:** Analyzing large datasets to generate insights for business decisions.
   - **Budget:** $2,500/month for tools and software.
  
2. **Persona 2: Jamie the Software Developer**
   - **Daily Job:** Building applications that require real-time data from various online sources.
   - **Budget:** $1,500/month for development tools and services.

3. **Persona 3: Taylor the Market Researcher**
   - **Daily Job:** Conducting market analysis by gathering data from competitor websites and industry reports.
   - **Budget:** $3,000/month for research tools and subscriptions.

## Channels
1. **Developer Forums and Communities (e.g., Stack Overflow, Reddit)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $50 per acquisition through targeted ads and sponsorships.
  
2. **Content Marketing (Blogs, Tutorials, Webinars)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $30 per acquisition through SEO and organic reach.

3. **Social Media Advertising (LinkedIn, Twitter)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $70 per acquisition through targeted ads to developers and analysts.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing Data Sifter + Social media teasers.
- **Week 2:** Publish a tutorial on web scraping basics + Share on developer forums.
- **Week 3:** Host a webinar on data extraction techniques using Data Sifter.
- **Week 4:** Release a case study showcasing Data Sifter's impact on a real project.
- **Week 5:** Share user testimonials and feedback on social media.
- **Week 6:** Publish an article on best practices for web scraping.
- **Week 7:** Create a video demo of Data Sifter in action.
- **Week 8:** Release a whitepaper on the importance of data extraction for businesses.
- **Week 9:** Conduct a Q&A session on social media to engage with potential users.
- **Week 10:** Share insights from the webinar and promote upcoming features.
- **Week 11:** Publish a comparison post of Data Sifter vs. competitors.
- **Week 12:** Recap the journey and announce upcoming updates and features.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and complete beta testing.
- **D-0:** Official launch of Data Sifter with a press release and promotional campaign.
- **D+30:** Analyze initial user feedback and make necessary adjustments.
- **D+90:** Evaluate product performance against success metrics and plan for future enhancements.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target of 1,000 DAU by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Target:** $50,000 by D+90.
